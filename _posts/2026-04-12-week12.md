## How to Make "Free" Profitable

While open source itself is not a business model that generates revenue, many successful business models are built around it. This week in class we discussed ways for open source projects and developers to make money in order to survive. For example, selling software as a service (SaaS) or Donations. We also talked about the Commodity vs. Differentiation rule, which argues that companies are wasting time and money on basic "plumbing" instead of creating something actually usefull. This is why many companies are joining in on open source projects in recent year. Many influencial projects such as WSL are being open sourced as big companies start using the Bazaar model instead of the cathedral model.
